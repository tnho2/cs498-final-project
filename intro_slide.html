Norwegian Development Fund Disbursements 2000-2015

The Norwegian Development Fund, an NGO started in 1978, supports small scale farmers in their fight against hunger and poverty.

https://www.utviklingsfondet.no/en

Their main areas of focus are:

Small scale farmers

Sustainable agriculture

Biological diversity

Political advocacy

Climate Adapted Villages

This visualization is an interactive slide show that will enable users to investigate where and how funds from the Norwegian Development Fund are being allocated.

Users can scroll down to reach the interactive slide show and final page. Click on the right and left arrows of the slide section to switch slides.
