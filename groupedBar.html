Norwegian Development Fund Disbursements 2000-2015

What type of projects were the funds used for in each region?

This chart shows what type of projects were funded by the disbursements from the Norwegian Fund between 2000-2015. The cumulative funds for each region are:

  • Africa             12,307,239
  • Asia                 6,093,064
  • America          3,400,477
  • Middle East    2,492,061
  • Europe            1,867,036
  • Oceania           18,119

Interactions

Hover over each bar to reveal the amount of funds spent on each target area per region.
